My Interests

  • Meditation

    Most people use meditation to solve their stress problems or otherwise help them relax, but my interest in it stems from a desire to explore the frontiers of my own mind. At risk of sounding too New Age-y, sinc I started practicing meditation I've noticed a profound increase in self awareness and attentiveness to my own thought patterns. Meditation has also assisted me in shaping these thought patterns and optimizing them for daily life.

  • Language Learning

    I've always been interested in languages and human communication. Currently, I'm a native speaker of English and I have a high degree of proficiency in French. I also have somewhat of an understanding of Hindi and Tamil. Over the course of my life, however, I've also been conversational in Russian (my abilities now are greatly diminished) and dabbled in German (which I still plan to learn in the future). Why? For no other reason than I love the process and the methods of learning foreign languages- to me, speaking other languages feels much like solving puzzles.

  • Exercising

    I've always enjoyed exercise, be it weight lifting, running, or hiking outside (admittedly, Urbana-Champaign doesn't provide for much of the latter). Right now I don't have any specific goal in mind, but I notice marked effects in my mood and energy levels in the days after a hard workout.

  • Reading

    Though I don't compare to any serious literary enthusiast, I do enjoy reading. In no particular order, some of my favorite books include The Picture of Dorian Gray by Oscar Wilde, The Little Prince by Antoine de Saint-Exupery (and I maintain that this is NOT a children's book), and biographies, although those depend much more on the subject and the author's capability.
